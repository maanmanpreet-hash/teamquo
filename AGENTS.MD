# Locked quote pricing preference

Material calculations are internal cost calculations only. They show the operator the material cost basis of a wall.

The final customer-facing Supply & Install price is manually entered per wall after reviewing the calculated material cost. The manual wall price includes labour, margin, difficulty, site conditions, travel, risk, and operator judgement.

Core rule:
- Material cost tells the operator what the job costs the business.
- Manual Supply & Install price is what the customer is charged.
- The customer quote total is the sum of manual wall-level Supply & Install prices.

Do not confuse internal material cost with customer-facing price.
Do not use calculated material cost as the final customer quote total.
Do not expose internal material costs, product-level prices, quantities, labour, margin, or markup on the customer-facing quote.
Do not redesign or alter the customer-facing quote unless explicitly requested.
